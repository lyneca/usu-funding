USU Funding Changes

The USU has changed student pricing for ACCESS membership:

  • $0 membership fee
  • optional $45 'discount' price (for a 10% discount campus-wide)

They have also cut funding for society events. Previously, societies were funded for $6 per ACCESS member for on-campus events, or $4 for off-campus events, up until breaking even. This is represented by the blue line on the graph to the right.

With the new funding scheme, societies are funded for 30% of the total event cost, plus $1 per USU member attending. This is represented by the red line.
